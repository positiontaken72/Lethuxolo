# Trusted care. Transforming lives for over 30 years.

At Hear and Say, we combine world-class hearing and speech expertise with a not-for-profit heart. From first sounds to lifelong conversations, we’ve been helping babies, children and adults connect with confidence for more than three decades.

[Our Services](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-services/)

We offer a range of specialised services to support hearing and communication for babies, children, and adults. Whether you or your child needs detailed hearing assessments, speech therapy, or early screening, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

## Why Choose Us

For over 30 years, families have trusted Hear and Say for personalised, supportive, and research-driven care.

### Family-Centered Approach

Ongoing guidance and support. Leaders in Listening and Spoken Language (LSL) therapy.

[Our Services](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-services/)

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-story-mission/)

#### Expertise

Accredited and qualified audiologists and speech pathologists.

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-services/)

#### Research Driven

Evidence-based practice backed by research.

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/resources-learning/?e-filter-888f155-category=research)

#### Inclusive programs for every stage of life

Personalised support for babies, children and adults.

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-services/resilience-wellbeing-programs/)

### Not-for-profit

Community-focused care. Strong partnerships with medical, educational and allied health professionals.

[About Us](https://hearandsay.org.au/about-us/)

[Read Our Mission](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-story-mission/)

#### Independent of hearing device manufacturers

We don’t jeopardise a clinical outcome for the sale of a hearing aid because we are a not-for-profit. Every sale of a hearing aid supports a child to hear.

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-services/hearing-aids/)

#### Medical Alliance

Hear and Say has aligned with ear nose and throat surgeons for 30 years creating a second generation of continuous care.

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-people/)

#### Approved Australian Government Hearing Services Provider

Hear and Say is accredited to provide fully subsidised hearing services, including hearing aids to eligible pensioners and veterans (DVA), under the Australian Government Hearing Services Program.

[Learn More](https://hearandsay.org.au/our-services/hearing-aids/)
