1. INTRODUCTION (OPENING STATEMENT)

Lethuxolo Trading is an emerging force within the South African mining and industrial services sector, positioned to deliver reliable, efficient, and scalable operational support across logistics, machinery, and infrastructure services.

Established in 2022 and based in Mpumalanga—one of South Africa’s key mining regions—the company is strategically aligned with the needs of heavy industry, particularly in coal, manganese, and steel-related operations.

From the outset, Lethuxolo Trading has been built with a clear objective:

To provide dependable, high-performance support services tailored for demanding industrial environments.

🧭 2. WHO WE ARE (COMPANY OVERVIEW)

Lethuxolo Trading operates at the intersection of logistics, mining support, and industrial services.

The company focuses on:

Bulk transportation

Supply chain support
Infrastructure and maintenance services

Rather than positioning itself as a general service provider, Lethuxolo Trading is structured as a specialized operational partner, designed to integrate seamlessly into mining and industrial workflows.

The company’s approach is rooted in:

Practical industry understanding
Operational discipline
Adaptability to client-specific requirements
⚙️ 3. WHAT WE DO (CORE SERVICES)

Lethuxolo Trading delivers a range of services essential to mining and industrial operations.

🚛 Bulk Transportation

Efficient and reliable transportation of coal, manganese, steel, and other bulk materials, ensuring consistent movement across supply chains.

🚜 Yellow Plant Machinery

Provision, rental, and operation of heavy-duty equipment including:

Excavators
Loaders
Dump trucks

These services support on-site operations where performance and uptime are critical.

🛣️ Road Maintenance

Maintenance and rehabilitation of mining and industrial roads, including grading, resurfacing, and repair to ensure operational continuity.

🧹 Industrial Cleaning

Professional cleaning services tailored to large-scale industrial environments, ensuring compliance, safety, and operational efficiency.

📦 Supply of Goods

Procurement and delivery of essential materials and equipment required for ongoing operations.

💡 Lighting Solutions

Provision of portable lighting plants to support night operations and low-visibility working conditions.
